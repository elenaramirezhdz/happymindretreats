About our founder

Meet our creative founder Elena. Elena is a professional certified coach and yoga teacher. She worked as a yoga teacher in different fitness clubs and studios in Berlin. She is accredited coach by the International Coaching Federation. She has her background in Communications and a Master of Arts in Languages and Communications. She has been living in different countries and speaks many Spanish, English, German and Russian fluently, she also speaks Portuguese and a bit of French. She is a creative, spiritual and active person. She loves helping people and motivating them to live to their fullest potential.
